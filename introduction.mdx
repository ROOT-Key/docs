## Who is ROOTKey?
ROOTKey is a cutting-edge cyber resilience company specializing in advanced digital security solutions. By leveraging technologies such as blockchain and zero-knowledge proofs. We ensures the highest levels of data integrity, privacy, and authenticity. 

Their products include decentralized asset maintenance, secure identity management, and comprehensive data protection services.

Operating within a zero-trust ecosystem, meaning that neither party in a transaction needs to rely on the other for security. This approach enhances overall security, increases the value and lifespan of digital and physical assets, and provides businesses with robust, scalable solutions to protect their sensitive information and operations.
