## What is ROOTKey?
We are changing the way you can protect your information and your customers!

In a reality where cyber-attacks on companies' systems and information are more and more frequent. Information is increasingly exposed, exposing and damaging many companies. 

ROOTKey works to automate and simplify the processes of validation, creation and management of your data and your customers'.
