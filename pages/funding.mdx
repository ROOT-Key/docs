This guide provides a step-by-step process for funding your wallet on the Polygon testnet using the Polygon Faucet.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the Polygon Faucet website" titleSize="h2">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="https://www.media.rootkey.ai/docs/fund1.png" />
    Visit [Polygon Faucet](https://faucet.polygon.technology/).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with Discord" titleSize="h2">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="https://www.media.rootkey.ai/docs/fund3.png" />
    Click on the "Log in with Discord" button and authenticate using your Discord
    account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the PoS (Amoy) Network" titleSize="h2">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="https://www.media.rootkey.ai/docs/fund4.png" />
    In the faucet, choose the PoS (Amoy) network, which corresponds to the testnet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste Your Wallet Address" titleSize="h2">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="https://www.media.rootkey.ai/docs/fund5.png" />
    Copy your wallet address and paste it into the designated field on the faucet
    page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the Request" titleSize="h2">
    <img style={{ borderRadius: "0.5rem" }} src="https://www.media.rootkey.ai/docs/fund6.png" />
    Click on the "Submit" button to request testnet funds. Your wallet should be
    funded shortly after.
  </Step>
</Steps>

By following these steps, you can easily fund your wallet on the Polygon testnet and start testing.
