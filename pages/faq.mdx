      <Accordion title="What does your product do?">
        ROOTKey's products provide comprehensive digital security solutions designed to ensure the integrity, privacy, and authenticity of data and digital assets. By leveraging advanced technologies such as blockchain and zero-knowledge proofs, ROOTKey offers [robust data protection](/api-reference/platform/overview). 
        
        These solutions enable businesses to maintain lifelong, immutable records of their assets, ensure secure and verifiable transactions, and protect sensitive information from unauthorized access. ROOTKey's products are designed to operate within a zero-trust ecosystem, ensuring that neither party in a transaction needs to rely on the other for security. 
        
        This approach not only enhances the overall security posture of businesses but also increases the value and lifespan of their digital and physical assets.
