## Notes

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No Return of Credits">
    There's no return of credits once they've been spent (even if you delete the wallet, the NFT, etc).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Gas Fee Responsibility">
    The user is responsible for paying the gas fee (and any costs associated with it), subject to exceptions in the enterprise plan.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Support Availability">
    5/8 support means support from 9am to 6pm weekdays.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Contact Method">
    The enterprise plan is tied to contacting the company via email, contact@rootkey.ai.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
